4.0ward Frontier:

Exploring the Future of Industry

21 July - 28 July 2024

Nis, Serbia

Step into the future of manufacturing right here in the captivating city of Nis, Serbia!

Industry 4.0, the fourth industrial revolution, is revolutionizing how we create, produce, and innovate. From IoT and robotics to data analytics and smart manufacturing, we'll demystify the core concepts and technologies driving Industry 4.0. Through engaging workshops, dynamic lectures, and hands-on activities, you'll gain a deep understanding of how these innovations are reshaping industries worldwide.

Equip yourself with essential skills to navigate the complexities of the forth industrial revolution with confidence. Learn how to harness the power of data analytics, embrace human-machine collaboration, and implement sustainable practices for a brighter, more resilient future.

Nestled in the heart of the Balkans, Nis is a city steeped in history and brimming with charm. It isn't just a city—it's a living testament to resilience, innovation, and cultural diversity.

As the birthplace of Roman Emperor Constantine the Great, Nis boasts a rich tapestry of architectural marvels, including the iconic Nis Fortress and the ancient Mediana archaeological site. With its vibrant markets, bustling cafes, and warm hospitality, Nis invites you to explore its hidden treasures and discover the true essence of Serbian culture.

Come join us and unlock the endless possibilities of Industry 4.0 in the heart of Nis!
